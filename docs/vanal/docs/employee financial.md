1. Executive Summary
   The "Financial Watchdog" is a new automated component within the CAFCA Intelligence Hub. Its purpose is to perform a real-time audit of every employee's labor distribution, instantly flagging profiles that deviate from financial health standards.

This document outlines the Logic Matrix currently implemented and requests approval for the specific percentage thresholds used to trigger alerts.

2. The Logic Matrix (Proposed)
   The system continuously monitors the "Selected Period" (e.g., Last Month, Last Quarter) and applies the following 3 rules.

🔴 Rule A: Critical Efficiency (The Red Flag)
Objective: Detect technicians who are costing the company money due to low productive hours.
Formula:
(Total Hours - (Transport + Loading)) / Total Hours
Current Threshold: < 60%
Trigger: If productive time drops below 60%, a CRITICAL alert is shown.
Context: This implies that for every 10 hours paid, less than 6 hours are billable/installing.
🟠 Rule B: High Transport (The Orange Flag)
Objective: Identify "masking" of downtime/inefficiency through excessive travel registration.
Formula: Transport Hours / Total Hours
Current Threshold: > 25%
Trigger: If driving time exceeds 25% of total time, a WARNING alert is shown.
Context: Industry standard for regional operations is typically 15-20%.
🟡 Rule C: Inactivity ( The Yellow Flag)
Objective: Identify "Silent" profiles (Ghost Workers).
Formula: Total Hours == 0
Trigger: If no hours are logged in the selected period.
